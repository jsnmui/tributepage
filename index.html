The Keyboard Cat

The Life of Fatso

  • Fatso was a female cat.
  • She was born in 1978.
  • Her owner was Charlie Schmidt of Spokane, Washington.
  • The first Keyboard Cat video was created by Schmidt in 1984.
  • Fatso died in 1987.
  • Schmidt had since adopted two other cats to become the new "Keyboard Cat" and providing video footage to be used for the meme: Bento until his death in 2018, and the new current Keyboard Cat, Skinny.
